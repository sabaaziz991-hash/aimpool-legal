AimPool Support

Help with AimPool: Billiards Shot Coach on iPhone and iPad.

Contact support

Open a support request and include your device model, iOS version, AimPool version, and the steps that led to the issue. Do not attach private photos unless they are necessary to explain the problem.

Open a support request

Purchases and restore

AimPool Premium is a one-time purchase handled by Apple. If Premium does not appear after purchase, open AimPool's purchase screen and use Restore Purchases. Confirm that the device is signed in with the Apple Account used for the original purchase.

Camera or photo access

If AimPool cannot access the camera or photo library, open iOS Settings, select AimPool, and review its Camera and Photos permissions.

Photo analysis

For best results, capture the full table from above when possible, keep every rail and pocket visible, reduce glare, and avoid motion blur. AimPool provides planning guidance; always verify the table before taking a shot.

Privacy

AimPool processes table photos on-device and does not upload them. Read the full Privacy Policy.
